Rajiv Gandhi Infotech Park:
A 2800-acre IT park in Hinjewadi, Rajiv Gandhi Infotech Park is one of the biggest IT park in India. Hinjewadi has become one of the poshest and prestigious localities in Pune owing to it becoming an IT hub. There are many famous multinational and national companies in this huge campus like Infosys, Tech Mahindra, Cognizant, KPIT Cummins Infosystems, etc.

Cybercity IT Park:
Magarpatta is a privately owned gated community located in Hadapsar area of Pune. Covering an area of 450-acres, Magarpatta city is one of the most prestigious townships in Pune. Cybercity IT park is located within this gated community and has some big companies like Accenture, Capgemini, Avaya, Red Hat, HCL, etc. based out of here.

EON Free Zone IT Park:
EON Free Zone located in Kharadi is one of the biggest IT parks in Pune. This IT Park covers an area of 45-acres and hosts some of the top companies like Vodafone, Barclays, Mphasis, FISglobal, etc. EON Free Zone provides you with world-class amenities and various options of eateries too.

Talawade IT Park:
Talawade is an industrial area in the Pimpri Chinchwad area of Pune. It is a well developed industrial area with a number of big industries. Talawade IT park or MIDC IT park hosts companies like Capgemini, Atos Syntel, Fujitsu, Sopra Steria, etc.

ICC Tech Park:
Located at the heart of the city, ICC Tech Park is one of the best IT parks in Pune. It is located in Shivajinagar and has a good combination of workspaces and social hubs. Some of the big companies present in ICC Tech Park are Deloitte, Cognizant, Nippon, Nokia, Force Motors, etc.

Cerebrum IT Park:
Located at the heart of the city, ICC Tech Park is one of the best IT parks in Pune. It is located in Shivajinagar and has a good combination of workspaces and social hubs. Some of the big companies present in ICC Tech Park are Deloitte, Cognizant, Nippon, Nokia, Force Motors, etc.
