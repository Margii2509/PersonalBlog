ITPL or International Tech Park , Bangalore:
The International Tech Park Bangalore, also known as ITPB, was established in 1992, at the height of Bangalore's tremendous IT Boom. It is also one of Bangalore's oldest tech parks and is the best there. ITPL is also one of the biggest IT parks in Bangalore. The Singapore and Indian governments collaborated to build the Tech Park.

Global Tech Park:
One of the biggest employers in the megapolis is Global Tech Park, one of the biggest IT parks in Bangalore, located in Electronic City. With hundreds of IT businesses and IT Parks, Electronic City is a hub that offers many job opportunities to its residents. Shriram Summit, Concorde Tech Turf, SNN Raj Greenbay, and other significant residential developments can be found close to Global Tech Park.

Prestige Tech Park:
The Marathahalli neighborhood of Bangalore's outer ring road is home to the Prestige Tech Park from the Prestige Group. The tech park is one of the best IT tech parks in Bangalore. Adobe, Bosch, Oracle, and other prominent businesses are located in Prestige Tech Park.

RMZ Infinity:
One of the biggest IT parks in Bangalore is RMZ Infinity. It was constructed in 2008 and is situated on Old Madras Road. Google, Thomson Reuters, and other renowned multinational companies have offices here. For businesses looking for a location between Whitefield and the Central Business District, RMZ Infinity frequently acts as a halfway point. The top residential developments close to RMZ Infinity include Sanctum, Assetz Sun, Shriram Blue, Durga Petals, etc.

Kirloskar Business Park:
One of the best IT parks in Bangalore is Kirloskar Business Park. The 8 Acre Marvel, which has seven floors is close to the Kempegowda International Airport. Vaishnavi North 24, Prestige Misty Waters, and other notable residential developments are located close to Kirloskar Business Park.

Brigade Tech Park:
Brigade Tech Park is located in East Bangalore's Whitefield neighborhood and tops the list of IT parks in Bangalore. Numerous IT firms, including Tata Consultancy Services, General Motors, Goldman Sachs, and others, are housed in its two main buildings. The housing complexes scattered throughout the neighborhood can reach it because its location is in the center of the neighborhood. Prestige Dolce Vista, Godrej Air, Incor Carmel Heights, and more top residential developments can be found close to Brigade Tech Park.
