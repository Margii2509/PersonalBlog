Wipro:
Wipro, one of India’s largest mobile app development companies in Kolkata, India, has established a strong foothold in Kolkata. Known for its expertise in IT consulting, business process outsourcing, and software development, Wipro has been at the forefront of driving digital transformation for numerous clients worldwide. The company’s emphasis on sustainable practices and its commitment to social responsibility make it stand out in the industry.

Cognizant Technology Solutions:
Cognizant, a multinational MVP development company in Kolkata, India. With a focus on digital transformation, cloud computing, and artificial intelligence, Cognizant delivers innovative solutions to its clients. The company’s global delivery model and its ability to adapt to changing market demands have positioned it as a leader in the IT industry.

Capgemini:
Capgemini, a leading startup development company in Kolkata, India. With a focus on digital transformation, cybersecurity, and cloud services, Capgemini offers a wide range of solutions to its clients. The company’s strong project management capabilities and its emphasis on innovation have made it a trusted partner for businesses across sectors.

HCL:
HCL needs no introduction. This company has ruled over the Indian IT industry since its inception in 1976. Hence, it makes sense to mention HCL in the list of best IT companies in Kolkata for jobs. The good thing about HCL is that it offers a good working environment to chill out and work.

TechMahindra:
A subsidiary of Mahindra Group, Tech Mahindra is ranked as no 5 IT firm in India. That’s why I have included this company in my list of IT companies in Kolkata. Working in Tech Mahindra will give you a good work-life balance as well as you’ll be working in a good environment where management is supportive.

Accenture:
With more than 492,000 employees, Accenture is next in this list of IT companies in Kolkata. Accenture follows an open working environment approach which creates a feeling of positivity among employees. Plus, they also offer pick and drop facility for their employees which sets them apart from other IT companies in Kolkata.
