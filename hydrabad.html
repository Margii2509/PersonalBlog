Raheja Mindspace, IT Park:
Raheja Mindspace is one of the largest and popular IT parks which is located in Madhapur, Hyderabad. Spread over 97 acres this IT Park is the house to topmost sectors like Biotech, Pharma, IT. This IT Park consists of top brand names like Cognizant, JP Morgan, Facebook India, Accenture, IBM and many more. It is a residential cum commercial hub with many IT software employees residing over here. Its efficient design, structure, and space give you a complete ambiance and business ecosystem which boasts a high level of productivity. Apart from all these, it has the best recreational facilities with food, banking, playing arena with the beauty of greenery which makes it a beautiful and livable place to work.

Cyber Pearl:
Located in Madhapur, Cyberpearl is also known as the heart of the growth center in Hyderabad. Cyberperal has managed to be in top IT parks in Hyderabad because of its all facilities like its food court, restaurants, conference rooms, and recreational facilities. Most importantly, It is the first IT park in Hyderabad to have its fully equipped health club and business center and it is also surrounded by so many good pubs and clubs for entertainment. Moreover, Cybera pearl is having top reputed international companies like Nexilabs, Keste, Damcosoft, etc.

CyberTowers:
This IT Park needs no introduction as it is one of the most iconic and popular IT parks in Hitech city, Hyderabad. Cyber towers are the first tower that was built in order to promote IT and its services. With its beautiful construction of buildings in quadrant form, it definitely has been the spark of Hitech City, Hyderabad. Moreover, It is the home to multinational companies such as Toshiba, L&T, Microsoft, Tata Teleservices and many more.

Divyasree NSL Orion:
Divyasree NSL Orion is a tech park which is located in Gachibowli, Hyderabad. With the growing revolution of IT and within a span of years, Gachibowli has emerged as one of the growing tech hubs for all the IT Companies in Hyderabad.

WaveRock SEZ:
Well, WaveRock SEZ is one of the most popular in Nanakramguda, Hyderabad which is the largest SEZ among all the IT parks. A perfect place to work because of its beautiful pathways, greenery environment, large cafeterias, and many more facilities. It is well connected with outer ring road and with all other facilities such as RTC Buses for transportation. Some of the well-known names like TCS, Accenture, Apple, Aveva, etc.

L&T Infocity:
L & t Infocity is the first software technology IT Park in Hyderabad. It is located in HITECH City, Hyderabad and is the place for well-known companies like Accenture, Microsoft, Oracle, etc.
